About Me;


Hello!
I am a student at Nile University of Nigeria, majoring in Computer Science. Even though, I study in this major, I am a self-taught artist, who started drawing at a very young age.
I had a passion for drawing since I was a little kid, and with the help of a friend, I constanly drew and eventually improved myself in this field.
First it started with color pencils then I learned to use more materials, now I enjoy watercolors and I can say that, coloring with them is so fun for me, so I can say that I am a watercolor artist.
However, I didn't stop there, once I saw the digital drawing enviroment, I became a fan of those artists and begun to work in that field as well. I can say I'm not profecional yet, but I'm still learning.
And at last, but not least, I've started to model 3D object and chracters using Blender app with the help of Rais Games.
